# hr-employee-attrition-analysis
HR Analytics project: predicting employee attrition using ML

## 📌 Overview

Employee attrition is one of the most costly challenges 
for organizations: replacing an employee can cost up to 
9 months of their salary in recruiting, onboarding, and 
training.

This project analyzes the IBM HR Analytics dataset — 1,470 
employees with 35 variables — to answer a concrete question:

> **Which factors predict employee attrition, and how can 
> a company intervene before it's too late?**

## 🎯 Objectives

- Identify the main risk factors driving attrition
- Build a predictive model (Random Forest + Logistic Regression)
- Translate findings into actionable recommendations for HR teams

## 🧠 Approach

The analysis combines data science techniques with a 
psychological lens: not just *what* the numbers predict, 
but *why* certain behavioral patterns lead to attrition.
